Title: Hottest day of the year recorded in UK, says Met Office
URL: https://www.theguardian.com/uk-news/2025/jul/01/hottest-day-of-the-year-recorded-in-uk-says-met-office

The UK’s hottest day of the year so far has been recorded with a temperature of at 33.6C at Frittenden in Kent, the Met Office has said.

The temperature exceeded the previous highest temperature of the year, which was 33.2C in Charlwood, Surrey on 21 June.

The UK Health Security Agency (UKHSA) extended amber heat health alerts for much of the country into Wednesday morning.
